About the Firm

Counsel You Can Rely On

Michael O. Ogunsakin & Co. is a general-practice law firm serving individuals, businesses, and public-sector clients from offices in Akure and Abuja. Our work spans litigation and dispute resolution, corporate and commercial advisory, real property transactions, family law, and regulatory compliance. We approach each matter with the attentiveness of a specialist and the breadth of a full-service chambers, drawing on a tradition of straightforward counsel and diligent representation.
